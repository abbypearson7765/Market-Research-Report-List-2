<p><strong>The Aluminum Die Casting Parts Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vacuum Die Casting</li><li>Pressure Die Casting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aluminum die casting parts market is divided into two main types: vacuum die casting and pressure die casting. Vacuum die casting involves the use of a vacuum to remove any air from the mold cavity before pouring the molten metal, ensuring a high-quality finish and reducing porosity. On the other hand, pressure die casting involves injecting the molten metal into the mold under high pressure to create intricate and complex designs, making it suitable for mass production. Both methods offer efficient and cost-effective solutions for manufacturing aluminum die casting parts.</p></p>
