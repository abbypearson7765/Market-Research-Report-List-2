<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>High-Tg PCB boards are categorized into three market types based on the glass transition temperature (Tg) values. The normal market includes boards with Tg values of 130℃, 135℃, and 140℃. The medium market comprises boards with Tg values of 150℃ and 155℃. The high market consists of boards with Tg values above 170℃. These categories represent the maximum temperature at which the board's material transitions from a glassy state to a rubbery state, ensuring improved stability and performance under high-temperature conditions.</p></p>
