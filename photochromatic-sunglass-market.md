<p><p>The photochromatic sunglass market can be categorized into three main types based on the material used for lenses. The first type is Mid-Index Plastic, which has a refractive index ranging from 1.53 to 1.65. This material offers good optical quality and is relatively affordable. The second type is High-Index Plastic, which has a refractive index above 1.65. This material is thinner and lighter than Mid-Index Plastic, making it suitable for those with higher prescription needs. Lastly, there is the Polycarbonate market, which offers impact resistance and is often preferred for sports and outdoor activities.</p></p>
