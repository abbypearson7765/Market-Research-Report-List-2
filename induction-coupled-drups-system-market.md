<p><p>The Induction Coupled DRUPS (Dynamic Rotary Uninterruptible Power Supply) System is an advanced power backup system used to provide uninterrupted power supply during power outages or fluctuations. It primarily consists of a diesel generator, a flywheel energy storage system, and an induction motor-generator. The system operates by converting mechanical energy from a continuously rotating flywheel into electrical energy during normal operation, and when a power outage occurs, the stored energy is then converted back into electrical energy to provide uninterrupted power supply.</p><p>The Induction Coupled DRUPS System Market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years. The increasing demand for reliable power supply in various industries such as data centers, healthcare, manufacturing, and telecommunications is driving the market growth.
