<p><p>Physical stimuli responsive polymers are materials that can change their properties in response to external stimuli, such as temperature, light, pressure, electricity, or magnetism. Thermo-responsive polymers can undergo reversible changes in their structure or solubility with temperature variations. Photo-responsive polymers can change their properties with exposure to light. Pressure-responsive polymers can change shape or volume under applied pressure. Electroactive polymers can exhibit electrical or mechanical response to an electric field. Magnetically-responsive polymers can undergo changes in properties when subjected to a magnetic field. These polymers have various applications in fields like medicine, biotechnology, and sensors.</p></p>
