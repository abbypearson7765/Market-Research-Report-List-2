<p><p>A Metal Oxide Varistor (MOV) is an electronic component typically used as a transient voltage suppressor. It is composed of a ceramic material formed into a disc or block shape and is made up of zinc oxide grains with small amounts of other metal oxide additives. MOVs are primarily designed to protect electrical and electronic devices from high voltage surges and transients by diverting the current away from the protected equipment.</p><p>The Metal Oxide Varistor market is expected to witness substantial growth during the forecast period. <p><p>The Metal Oxide Varistor (MOV) market can be categorized into two types: SMD Type and Disc Type varistors. SMD Type MOVs are surface-mounted devices designed for compact electronic circuits, offering high accuracy and reliability. On the other hand, Disc Type MOVs are widely used in power and surge protection applications. They provide robust protection against voltage surges and transient overvoltages due to their high energy absorption capabilities. Both types of varistors serve the purpose of protecting electronic devices from electrical disturbances, but differ in their form factor and specific applications.</p></p>
