<p><p>The direct-coat IR glazing market consists of two types: monolayer and multilayer. Monolayer IR glazing is a single layer coating applied to the glazing surface. It provides basic heat insulation and infrared reflection properties. On the other hand, multilayer IR glazing is composed of multiple layers of coatings, offering superior heat insulation, infrared reflection, and energy efficiency. Multilayer IR glazing provides enhanced thermal performance and is commonly used in energy-efficient building designs. Both types are used to reduce heat transfer and improve energy efficiency in various applications.</p></p>
