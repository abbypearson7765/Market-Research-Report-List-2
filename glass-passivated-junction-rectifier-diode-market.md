<p><p>Glass Passivated Junction Rectifier Diode Market Types include PN Diode, Schottky Barrier Diode, Fast Recovery Diode, and Others. </p><p>A PN Diode is a traditional diode that allows current to flow in one direction and blocks it in the opposite direction. It is commonly used in rectification and switching circuits.</p><p>A Schottky Barrier Diode has a metal-semiconductor junction, providing lower forward voltage drop and faster switching speed compared to PN diodes. It is suitable for high-frequency applications.</p><p>A Fast Recovery Diode has a quick recovery time, allowing it to switch between conducting and blocking states rapidly. It is commonly used in switching power supplies and inverters.</p><p>The "Others" category represents various diode types that may not fit into the above categories, such as Zener diodes or light-emitting diodes (LEDs).</p></p>
