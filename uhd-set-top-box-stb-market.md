<p><p>The UHD set-top box (STB) market encompasses various types of technologies including cable, satellite, DTT (Digital Terrestrial Television), IP (Internet Protocol), and OTT (Over-The-Top). Cable STBs are used in cable television systems to deliver TV signals through coaxial cables. Satellite STBs receive signals from satellite broadcasts. DTT STBs receive digital signals from terrestrial broadcast networks. IP STBs utilize internet protocol to deliver TV content over internet connections. OTT STBs provide streaming services over the internet without the need for traditional broadcast networks. These different market types cater to diverse consumer preferences and technological capabilities.</p></p>
