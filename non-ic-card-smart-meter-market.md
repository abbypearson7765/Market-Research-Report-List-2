<p><p>Non-IC card smart meters can be divided into two types: single phase meters and three phase meters. Single phase meters are typically used in residential households and are designed to measure the electricity consumption of individual homes. Three phase meters, on the other hand, are used in larger commercial or industrial settings that require a higher electrical load. These meters are capable of measuring the consumption of three-phase electrical power, which is common in such settings. Both types of meters are part of the non-IC card smart meter market, catering to different electricity consumption needs.</p></p>
