<p><p>Active infrared sensors are used in various industries for motion detection and proximity sensing. Quantum infrared sensors use quantum tunneling to detect objects and are commonly used in security systems, burglar alarms, and automatic doors. Both types of active infrared sensors provide reliable and accurate detection capabilities, with thermal sensors focusing on heat and quantum sensors relying on quantum phenomena for object detection.</p></p>
