<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Buttermilk powder is available in two different market types: spray dried buttermilk powder and atmospheric roller dried buttermilk powder. Spray dried buttermilk powder is produced by spraying liquid buttermilk under high pressure and high temperature, which instantly turns it into powder form. This method helps in preserving the nutrients and flavor of the buttermilk. On the other hand, atmospheric roller dried buttermilk powder is produced by spreading buttermilk onto a heated drum, where it is dried and then scraped off in the form of flakes. This method provides a slightly different texture and flavor to the buttermilk powder.</p></p>
