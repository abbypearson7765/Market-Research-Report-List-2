<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The tunable filter market consists of various types of filters. Liquid Crystal Tunable Filters (LCTFs) use liquid crystals and are capable of easily tuning the center wavelength. Acousto-Optic Tunable Filters (AOTFs) use sound waves to control the transmitted wavelength. Linear-Variable Tunable Filters (LVTFs) use a linearly varying transmission characteristic with wavelength. Other types of tunable filters include Fabry-Perot interferometers and Lyot filters. These filters are used in applications such as spectroscopy, telecommunications, and biomedical imaging, offering flexibility and versatility in selecting desired wavelengths.</p></p>
