<p><p>RF modulators are devices used to convert baseband signals into radio frequency signals for transmission. In the market, there are various types of RF modulators available. IQ modulators modulate baseband signals using in-phase and quadrature components. QPSK modulators enable four different phase states to transmit data. Bi-Phase modulators use a balanced phase-shift keying technique. Quadrature modulators combine two signals to create a single RF signal. BPSK modulators use binary phase shift keying for signal transmission. Besides these, there are other types of modulators available in the market, each offering unique features and capabilities.</p></p>
