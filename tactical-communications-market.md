<p><p>Tactical Communications market includes several types of equipment used in military operations. Soldier Radio is a compact and portable communication device that enables individual soldiers to communicate with each other. Manpack Radio provides similar capabilities but with a longer range and increased power. VIC refers to Vehicular Intercommunication Radio, designed for communication between vehicles in the field. High Capacity Data Radio (HCDR) facilitates high-speed data transfer for real-time situational awareness. Situational awareness video receiver allows soldiers to receive live video feeds for better understanding of the surroundings. Rugged networking devices support secure and reliable communication in harsh environments.</p></p>
