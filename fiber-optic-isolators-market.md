<p><p>Fiber optic isolators are devices used to prevent the backward transmission of light signals in fiber optic systems, protecting the components from damage. Sensitive isolators are primarily designed to work with low-power applications, offering high isolation ratios and low insertion losses. They are ideal for applications such as telecommunications and medical instruments. On the other hand, insensitive isolators are designed to operate in high-power applications, providing robust isolation and high power handling capability. They are commonly used in laser systems, optical amplifiers, and high-power fiber lasers.</p></p>
