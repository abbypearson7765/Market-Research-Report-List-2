<p><p>Solar lamps are lighting devices that use the sun's energy to provide illumination. The market for solar lamps includes different types, such as Compact Fluorescent Lamps (CFL) and Light Emitting Diodes (LED). CFL solar lamps are energy-efficient and emit light by using electric current to excite gases within the lamp. On the other hand, LED solar lamps produce light by passing an electric current through a semiconductor material, making them even more energy-efficient and durable. Both types of solar lamps have gained popularity due to their sustainability and ability to reduce electricity consumption.</p></p>
