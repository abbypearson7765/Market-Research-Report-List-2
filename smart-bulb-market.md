<p><p>A smart bulb, also known as a connected bulb or smart light, is an LED light bulb that can be controlled remotely through a smartphone, tablet, or other smart devices. The market is witnessing a shift from traditional lighting to smart lighting systems, driven by the benefits offered by smart bulbs, including energy savings, convenience, and improved ambiance.</p><p>The market analysis indicates that North America dominates the smart bulb market, followed by Europe and the Asia Pacific. <p><p>The Smart Bulb market is segmented into various types such as ZigBee, Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, and Others. ZigBee smart bulbs utilize the ZigBee wireless communication standard for seamless connectivity and efficient energy management. Wi-Fi smart bulbs connect directly to the existing Wi-Fi network, allowing remote control through mobile devices. Bluetooth smart bulbs connect to smartphones or tablets via a Bluetooth connection for convenient control. Finally, the Other segment includes smart bulbs that utilize different technologies like Z-Wave, Thread, or proprietary protocols, offering diverse options for users seeking smart lighting solutions.</p></p>
