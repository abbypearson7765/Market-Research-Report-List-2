<p><p>The smartphone microphone market is divided into two main types: electric microphones and condenser microphones. Electric microphones utilize electromagnetic induction to convert sound waves into electrical signals. They are commonly used in smartphones due to their simplicity and reliability. On the other hand, condenser microphones employ a capacitor to convert sound vibrations into electrical signals. These microphones are known for their high sensitivity and accurate audio reproduction. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing the smartphone user experience and ensuring clear and high-quality voice recordings or calls.</p></p>
