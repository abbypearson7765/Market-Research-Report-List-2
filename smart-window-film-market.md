<p><p>Smart window film is a technologically advanced product used to control the amount of light and heat entering through windows. It is available in two primary market types: self-adhesive film and non-adhesive film. Self-adhesive film simply means that it can be directly applied to the window surface using an adhesive layer. On the other hand, non-adhesive film does not require any adhesive and uses static cling or other techniques to stick to the window. Both types provide the same functionality of regulating light, heat, and privacy, but differ in how they are applied to the windows.</p></p>
