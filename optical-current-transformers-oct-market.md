<p><p>Optical Current Transformers (OCT) are devices used to measure electric currents. There are four types in the market. Magneto Optical Current Transformers (MOCT) utilize Faraday effect and magneto-optic material to measure the current. Fiber Optical Current Transformers (FOCT) utilize optical fibers to transmit the signal. Electronic Fiber Optical Current Transformers (EFOCT) incorporate electronic components to enhance performance. Flexible Electronic Fiber Optical Current Transformers (FEFOCT) are based on EFOCT but offer flexibility for easy installation. These OCT types provide accurate current measurements and have diverse applications in various industries.</p></p>
