<p><p>A fluxgate current sensor is a type of current sensor that utilizes the principle of magnetic fluxgate to measure electrical current. It consists of a magnetic core made of a highly permeable material, which is subjected to an alternating magnetic field generated by the electrical current under measurement. The core's magnetic properties change in response to the current, causing changes in the magnetic field, which are then detected and converted into an electrical signal proportional to the current.</p><p>The future outlook for the fluxgate current sensor market is promising.
