<p><p>The Pluggable Optical Module market consists of four main types: Optical Receiver Modules, Optical Transmitter Modules, Optical Transceiver Modules, and Optical Transponder Modules. Optical Receiver Modules are responsible for receiving optical signals and converting them into electrical signals. Optical Transmitter Modules, on the other hand, convert electrical signals into optical signals for transmission. Optical Transceiver Modules combine both functionalities, allowing for bidirectional communication. Lastly, Optical Transponder Modules can convert optical signals from one wavelength to another, facilitating compatibility between different optical network systems.</p></p>
