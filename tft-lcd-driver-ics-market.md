<p><p>TFT LCD Driver ICs are a type of integrated circuit that is used to drive the Thin Film Transistor (TFT) display panels commonly found in electronic devices like smartphones and TVs. There are two main types of TFT LCD Driver ICs: Source Driver and Gate Driver. Source Driver ICs control the current flowing from the power source to the pixel. On the other hand, Gate Driver ICs control the voltage applied to the TFT array, enabling the transmission of signals between the display and the controller. Both types of Driver ICs play crucial roles in ensuring optimal performance and image quality of TFT displays.</p></p>
