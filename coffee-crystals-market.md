<p><strong>The Coffee Crystals Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spray Drying</li><li>Freeze Drying</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Coffee crystals are a popular form of instant coffee that comes in two market types: spray drying and freeze drying. Spray drying involves spraying a liquid coffee extract into a hot air chamber, resulting in the formation of coffee crystals. This method is cost-effective and widely used in the industry. In contrast, freeze drying involves freezing the liquid coffee extract and then removing the ice through sublimation, resulting in coffee crystals. Although more expensive, freeze drying retains the coffee's aroma and flavor better, making it a preferred choice for some consumers.</p></p>
