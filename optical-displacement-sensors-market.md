<p><ul><li>PSD Method</li><li>CMOS (CCD) Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical displacement sensors are widely used in various industries for measuring distance or position. The market includes two main types: PSD method and CMOS (CCD) method. In the PSD method, the sensor utilizes a position-sensitive detector to convert light intensity into an electrical signal, offering precise measurements. On the other hand, CMOS (CCD) method employs complementary metal-oxide-semiconductor or charge-coupled device technology to capture light, providing high-resolution images for displacement tracking. Both methods play a crucial role in delivering accurate and reliable optical displacement sensing solutions for different applications.</p></p>
