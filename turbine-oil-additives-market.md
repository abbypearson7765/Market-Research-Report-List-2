<p><p>Turbine oil additives consist of two main types: single component and additive package. Single component additives refer to individual chemicals that enhance the performance of turbine oils by combating issues like oxidation, foam, and rust. On the other hand, additive packages are a combination of different chemicals blended together to provide a comprehensive solution for improving turbine oil performance. These packages may include additives such as anti-wear agents, detergents, anti-foaming agents, and viscosity index improvers, among others. Both single component additives and additive packages play crucial roles in enhancing the quality and efficiency of turbine oils.</p></p>
