<p><p>The Fruit & Vegetable Enzymes Market comprises different types of enzymes such as pectinase, amylase, protease, and cellulase. Pectinase is responsible for breaking down pectin, a compound found in fruit and vegetables that helps in the formation of jellies and jams. Amylase assists in the breakdown of starches, while protease breaks down proteins into amino acids. Lastly, cellulase helps in the breakdown of cellulose, a complex carbohydrate found in plants. These enzymes play a crucial role in various food processing industries by aiding in the breakdown of specific components.</p></p>
