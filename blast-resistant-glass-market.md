<p><p>Blast Resistant Glass, also known as explosion-proof glass or ballistic glass, is a specialized type of glass designed to withstand the impact of explosions. It is made by sandwiching multiple layers of glass with a thick interlayer material like polyvinyl butyral (PVB) or polycarbonate. The layers of glass and interlayer material prevent the glass from shattering and provide enhanced resistance against shock waves, debris, and flying glass fragments.</p><p>The Blast Resistant Glass Market is expected to experience significant growth during the forecast period. One of the main driving factors behind this growth is the increasing security concerns across various industries. Governments, public institutions, and private organizations are investing heavily in strengthening their infrastructure's safety and security measures, including blast-resistant glass installations.</p><p>The market is also witnessing a rise in demand from the automotive industry. <p><p>Blast resistant glass is divided into two main types: Ordinary Blast Resistant Glass and Reinforced Blast Resistant Glass.
