<p><p>The headphones market can be divided into three types: in-ear, over-ear, and on-ear. In-ear headphones are designed to fit snugly inside the ear canal, providing good noise isolation and portability. Over-ear headphones enclose the ears completely and tend to offer better sound quality and comfort for extended use. On-ear headphones sit on top of the ears and provide good sound quality while being more portable than over-ear headphones. Each type has its own advantages and caters to different needs and preferences of consumers.</p></p>
