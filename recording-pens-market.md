<p><p>Recording pens are commonly available in three market types - pen-type, rod-type, and others. </p><p>Pen-type recording pens are designed to resemble a regular pen, making them inconspicuous and easy to carry. They are suitable for covert audio recording in meetings, interviews, or for personal use.</p><p>Rod-type recording pens are longer in shape, similar to a rod or stick. They often have a built-in microphone at one end and are used for capturing audio in larger spaces, such as classrooms, lectures, or public events.</p><p>The "others" category includes recording pens that differ from the typical pen or rod shapes, such as those designed as wearables or have unique features tailored for specific recording purposes.</p></p>
