<p><p>Rotary angle sensors are devices used for measuring the angular position or rotation of an object. They are available in different types for various applications. Rotary potentiometers are resistive sensors that change resistance based on the angular position. Resolvers use electromagnetic coupling for angle measurement. Rotary encoders convert rotation into digital signals through optical or magnetic sensing. Rotary variable differential transformers detect the angular displacement using electromagnetic induction. These sensors find application in industries like automotive, aerospace, and robotics for accurate position sensing and control purposes.</p></p>
