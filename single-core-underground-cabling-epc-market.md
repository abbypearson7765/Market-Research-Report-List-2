<p><p>The single core underground cabling EPC market is divided into three main types: PVC, XLPE, and rubber. PVC refers to cables that have a polyvinyl chloride insulation, which provides moderate thermal resistance and ensures durability. XLPE cables have cross-linked polyethylene insulation, providing higher thermal resistance and excellent electrical properties. Rubber cables are made of synthetic rubber insulation, making them flexible and suitable for harsh environments. All three types of cables are used for underground power transmission and distribution, with variations in their features and performance.</p></p>
