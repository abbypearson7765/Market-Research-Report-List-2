<p><strong>The Thermally Conductive PU Adhesive Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Component</li><li>Multi Component</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thermally conductive PU adhesive market includes two types: single component and multi-component adhesives. Single component adhesives are pre-mixed and ready to use, making them convenient for quick applications. They typically cure at room temperature. On the other hand, multi-component adhesives require two or more components to be mixed before use. They often require curing at elevated temperatures. The choice between these two types depends on the specific application requirements, such as curing time, temperature resistance, and bonding strength.</p></p>
