<p><ul><li>Single-blade beam shutter</li><li>Multiple-blade beam shutter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Beam shutters are devices used to control the direction and intensity of light beams. Single-blade beam shutters have a single blade that moves in front of the light source to block or allow light to pass through. They are commonly used in applications requiring simple beam control. On the other hand, multiple-blade beam shutters consist of multiple blades that can move independently, allowing more precise and complex beam control. They are usually used in advanced lighting systems or applications where greater control over the light beam is needed.</p></p>
