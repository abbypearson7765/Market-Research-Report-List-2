<p><strong>The Cassette Air Conditioner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Out Wind</li><li>Two Out Wind</li><li>Four Out Wind</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cassette air conditioners come in three main types: single out wind, two out wind, and four out wind. In a single out wind system, the air is released through a single opening in the center of the unit. Two out wind systems have two separate openings on opposite sides, providing a more balanced air distribution. Four out wind systems disperse the air through four openings positioned in each corner of the unit, allowing for maximum airflow and cooling efficiency. These different types offer varying levels of air distribution and coverage for different room sizes and cooling needs.</p></p>
