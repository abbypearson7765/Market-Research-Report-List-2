<p><strong>The Ultrasound Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Beam Sensors</li><li>Proximity Sensors</li><li>Ultrasonic Retro-Reflective Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ultrasound sensors are used to detect distance, level, and presence by emitting sound waves and analyzing their reflections. Beam sensors operate by emitting narrow beams that are interrupted by an object, triggering a response. Proximity sensors use a wide beam to detect the presence of an object within a specified range. Ultrasonic retro-reflective sensors emit sound waves towards a reflector and detect the reflection to determine the presence or absence of an object. These sensor types find applications across various industries for accurate and efficient object detection and distance measurement.</p></p>
