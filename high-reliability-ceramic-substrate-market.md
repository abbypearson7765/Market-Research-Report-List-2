<p><p>The High-Reliability Ceramic Substrate market comprises two types: HTCC Substrate and LTCC Substrate. HTCC Substrate refers to High-Temperature Co-Fired Ceramic substrates, which are used in applications demanding high thermal stability and reliability. These substrates have excellent electrical insulation properties. On the other hand, LTCC Substrate stands for Low-Temperature Co-Fired Ceramic substrates. They are used in applications requiring high integration and miniaturization capabilities. LTCC substrates offer good electrical performance and are suitable for high-frequency applications. Both types of substrates play a crucial role in various industries such as electronics, telecommunications, and automotive.</p></p>
