<p><p>Snow goggles come in various types to cater to different vision needs. Ordinary lenses are suitable for individuals with normal vision, providing protection against snow glare and UV rays. Myopic lenses are designed specifically for nearsighted people, correcting their vision while shielding their eyes from the elements. These market segments address the diverse vision requirements to enhance safety and visual performance while engaged in snowy environments.</p></p>
