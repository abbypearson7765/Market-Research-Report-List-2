<p><strong>The Edge Routers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Subscriber Edge Router</li><li>Label Edge Router</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Edge routers are an essential component of network infrastructure, catering to different market needs. Subscriber Edge Routers specifically focus on connecting individual users or subscriber networks to the internet, enabling access to various services and applications. On the other hand, Label Edge Routers are used in Multiprotocol Label Switching (MPLS) networks. They efficiently direct data packets by assigning labels rather than complex routing tables, ensuring faster and more reliable transfer. These market types serve distinct purposes, targeting end-users and optimizing data flow within networks, respectively.</p></p>
