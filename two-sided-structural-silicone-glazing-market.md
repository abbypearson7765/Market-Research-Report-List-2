<p><p>Two-sided Structural Silicone Glazing (SSG) is a glazing technique commonly used in the construction industry. The market types associated with this technique are aluminum structural framing, glass panel, silicone sealant, and ethylene propylene diene monomer (EPDM) market. Aluminum structural framing involves using aluminum frames to support the glass panels in two-sided SSG. Glass panels are the main component of this glazing technique, providing transparency and insulation. Silicone sealant is used to bond the glass panels to the aluminum frames, ensuring a secure fit. EPDM market refers to the use of ethylene propylene diene monomer gaskets to provide weatherproofing and seal the joints in two-sided SSG.</p></p>
