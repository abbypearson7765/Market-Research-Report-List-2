<p><strong>The CO2 Gas Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>NDIR CO2 Gas Sensor</li><li>Catalytic CO2 Gas Sensor</li><li>Thermal Conductivity CO2 Gas Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>NDIR (Non-Dispersive Infrared) CO2 gas sensors measure the concentration of CO2 by analyzing the absorption of infrared light. They are commonly used in indoor air quality monitoring and industrial applications due to their high accuracy and stability. Catalytic CO2 gas sensors utilize a catalytic reaction to detect CO2 levels, commonly used in automotive exhaust systems to measure emissions. Thermal conductivity CO2 gas sensors measure changes in thermal conductivity to determine CO2 content, often used in applications like HVAC systems and breath analyzers.</p></p>
