<p><p>The Artificial Intelligence Chips market encompasses various types such as GPU, ASIC, FPGA, and CPU. GPUs (Graphics Processing Units) are specialized chips often used for parallel processing, enabling faster AI computations. ASICs (Application-Specific Integrated Circuits) are designed for specific AI tasks, enhancing performance and energy efficiency. FPGAs (Field-Programmable Gate Arrays) offer flexible configurability, making them suitable for diverse AI applications. CPUs (Central Processing Units) are general-purpose chips that support a wide range of tasks, including AI computations. Each type caters to specific requirements, balancing factors like speed, power efficiency, and adaptability in the AI hardware landscape.</p></p>
