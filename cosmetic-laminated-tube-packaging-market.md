<p><strong>The Cosmetic Laminated Tube Packaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ABL</li><li>PBL</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cosmetic laminated tube packaging has two main types in the market: ABL (Aluminum Barrier Laminate) and PBL (Plastic Barrier Laminate). ABL tubes are made by laminating aluminum and plastic layers together, providing excellent barrier properties against moisture, oxygen, and light. This type of tube offers enhanced protection for sensitive cosmetic products. PBL tubes are made entirely of plastic, providing good barrier properties while being lightweight and flexible. They are cost-effective and widely used for various cosmetic products. Both types of laminated tubes are popular choices for packaging cosmetic products due to their durability and protective features.</p></p>
