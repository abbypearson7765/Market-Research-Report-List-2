<p><p>The airport e-gates market consists of three main types: fully automatic, semi-automatic, and other markets. In fully automatic e-gates, passengers complete the entire process themselves, including identity verification and immigration control. Semi-automatic e-gates require minimal passenger involvement, where they still need to present documents for verification but the gate operates automatically. The "other" market encompasses various gate types that do not fit into the fully or semi-automatic categories, such as manual gates or advanced biometric systems. These different market types offer flexibility and efficiency in airport operations, ensuring smooth and secure passenger flow.</p></p>
