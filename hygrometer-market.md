<p><strong>The Hygrometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Capacitive Hygrometer</li><li>Resistive Hygrometer</li><li>Thermal Hygrometer</li><li>Gravimetric Hygrometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hygrometers are instruments used to measure humidity levels in the air. The market types of hygrometers include capacitive, resistive, thermal, and gravimetric hygrometers. Capacitive hygrometers use a capacitance sensor to measure humidity. Resistive hygrometers rely on changes in electrical resistance to determine humidity levels. Thermal hygrometers utilize the cooling effect of evaporation to assess humidity. Gravimetric hygrometers measure humidity by analyzing weight changes in an adsorbent material. These different types cater to various applications and requirements, allowing users to select the most suitable hygrometer for their specific needs.</p></p>
