<p><p>Bottle nipples are categorized into different types according to the flow rate they offer. Slow flow nipples have a smaller hole size, making them suitable for newborns and infants in the early stages of feeding. Medium flow nipples have a slightly larger hole size, providing a comfortable pace for older infants. Fast flow nipples have larger openings, allowing a faster flow for more experienced eaters. Variable flow nipples have adjustable flow rates, which parents can control according to their baby's needs, offering versatility and convenience. Each market type fulfills specific feeding requirements during a baby's development.</p></p>
