<p><strong>The Color CMOS Image Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Global Shutter</li><li>Rolling Shutter</li></ul></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The Color CMOS Image Sensors market consists of two types: Global Shutter and Rolling Shutter. Global Shutter technology captures an entire image simultaneously, resulting in accurate and distortion-free images even in fast-paced scenarios, making it ideal for applications like sports photography and autonomous vehicles. On the other hand, the Rolling Shutter technology captures images by scanning row by row, which can lead to distortion in fast-moving objects. It is commonly used in applications such as video recording and mobile photography, where cost and power consumption are critical factors.</p></p>
