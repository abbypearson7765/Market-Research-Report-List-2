<p><p>The high resolution monitor market includes various types such as 1280x720 (720p), 1920x1080 (1080i), 1920x1080 progressive (1080p), and 2560x1440. The 720p resolution offers a display of 1280x720 pixels, whereas the 1080i provides a resolution of 1920x1080 but interlaces the lines to create an image. The 1080p resolution also delivers 1920x1080 pixels but progressively scans the lines, resulting in a smoother image. The 2560x1440 resolution offers even higher clarity with a display of 2560x1440 pixels, making it ideal for precise detailed work or high-quality media consumption.</p></p>
